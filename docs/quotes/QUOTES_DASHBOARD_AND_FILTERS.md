# Quotes Dashboard & Filters

## Dashboard Widgets
- Total quotes this month
- Acceptance rate (accepted / sent)
- Expiring soon (next 7 days)
- Revenue at stake (sum of sent but not decided)

## Saved Views
- “Expiring in 7 days” (Sent with validity in next 7 days)
- “Awaiting response” (Sent/Viewed but not Accepted/Declined)
- “Viewed” (viewedAt exists)
- “Declined (30 days)” (Declined within last 30 days)

## Filters & Search
- Status, validity date range, created date range
- Customer, project/job
- Amount range, currency, language
- Numbering pattern, quote number
- Sender/user

## List Usability
- Pagination with quick totals
- Bulk actions: resend, export CSV/PDF bundle
