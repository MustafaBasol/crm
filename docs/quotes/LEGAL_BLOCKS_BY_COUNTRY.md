# Legal Blocks by Country (Guidance)

This is guidance only. Consult legal counsel for exact wording.

## United States (US)
- Applicable taxes: Sales tax notes per state/jurisdiction
- Right of withdrawal: Not generally applicable for B2B; include cancellation terms if offered
- Dispute venue: Company’s chosen state/county
- Identification: EIN on PDF

## France (FR)
- Applicable taxes: TVA details and rates
- Mediation: Include consumer mediation info if B2C; for B2B, specify applicable mediation if any
- Dispute venue: Tribunal de Commerce city
- Identification: SIRET/SIREN, TVA intracommunautaire

## Germany (DE)
- Applicable taxes: USt (Mehrwertsteuer) details and rates
- Withdrawal: For B2C, Widerrufsrecht per law; for B2B, clarify contract terms
- Dispute venue: Gerichtsstand (city)
- Identification: USt-IdNr., Handelsregisternummer

## Türkiye (TR)
- Applicable taxes: KDV oranları; Tevkifat (withholding) notu gerektiğinde
- Cayma hakkı: B2C sözleşmelerinde mesafeli satış yönetmeliğine uygun metin
- Uyuşmazlık: İhtilaf halinde yetkili mahkeme/ICRA dairesi
- Kimlik: Vergi No, MERSIS (varsa)

## Bank & Payment Footers
- Bank name, IBAN/ABA, SWIFT/BIC; payment terms (e.g., 30 days), early payment discounts or late fees if applicable
